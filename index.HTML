AUTOMOTIVE CAR PARTS.

The system provides a structured way to store and retrieve information related to the inventory of car parts, suppliers, associated cars, and orders. made for parts. It uses SQL to perform various operations, such as retrieving data, analysing, orders, and linking parts to cars and suppliers. The objective of this project is to design a relational database to simulate an inventory and order management system for the automotive industry. The database enables seamless tracking of car parts, their suppliers, the cars they are compatible with, and orders placed for these parts.
